Essay Writing Tips

Start Strong: Your opening sentence should grab the reader's attention immediately. Use a personal story, surprising fact, or compelling question.

Show, Don't Tell: Instead of saying "I'm hardworking," describe a specific situation where you demonstrated hard work and its impact.

Be Authentic: Scholarship committees read thousands of essays. What makes yours stand out is your unique voice and genuine passion.

Proofread Carefully: Errors distract from your message. Read aloud, use spell check, and ask someone else to review your essay.
