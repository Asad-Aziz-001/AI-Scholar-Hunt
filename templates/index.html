About AI Scholar Hunt

Pakistan's First AI-Powered Scholarship Platform

AI Scholar Hunt is a Final Year Project built to solve a real problem — thousands of Pakistani students miss out on fully funded international scholarships simply because they don't know where to look or how to apply.

We built an intelligent platform that combines AI-powered recommendations, a RAG-based chatbot, and smart application tools to guide students from discovery all the way to submission.
